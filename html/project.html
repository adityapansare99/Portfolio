Mission Pankh: Giving Wings to the T-1 Diabetes Patients

Mission Pankh is an innovative solution developed to provide better management for Type-1 Diabetes (T-1 Diabetes) patients. The primary goal of the project is to assist diabetes patients in monitoring their health and provide them with tools to better control and improve their condition. The project integrates various technologies to offer real-time health tracking and personalized recommendations, aiming to enhance the overall quality of life for T-1 Diabetes patients.

Technologies Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, GIT
